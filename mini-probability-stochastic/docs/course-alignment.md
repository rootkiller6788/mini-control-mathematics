## Common Core Intersection

The intersection of all nine curricula identifies these mandatory topics
(all covered by this module):

1. Axioms of probability, random variables, distributions (L1)
2. Expectation, variance, conditional probability, Bayes theorem (L2-L3)
3. Law of large numbers, central limit theorem (L4)
4. Maximum likelihood estimation (L5)
5. Markov chains — discrete and continuous time (L1-L2)
6. Poisson process and Brownian motion (L1-L2)
7. Monte Carlo methods — integration and sampling (L5)
8. Kalman filtering — state estimation (L5)
9. Information theory basics — entropy, KL divergence (L5)
10. Bootstrap and resampling methods (L5-L7)
